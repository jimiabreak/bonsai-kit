# About Commonwealth Coffee

## Our Story

Commonwealth Coffee was founded with a simple mission: to bring exceptional coffee and genuine community together. What started as a small roastery has grown into a beloved neighborhood gathering place.

## Our Coffee

We're passionate about sourcing the finest beans from sustainable farms around the world. Each batch is carefully roasted in-house to highlight the unique characteristics of the origin.

### Roasting Philosophy

- **Small Batch**: We roast in small batches to ensure consistency and freshness
- **Sustainable**: We partner with farms that prioritize environmental stewardship
- **Traceable**: Every bag can be traced back to its origin

## The Space

Our café is designed to be a welcoming space for everyone. Whether you're here for a quick espresso or settling in for an afternoon of work, we want you to feel at home.

## Community

We believe coffee is better when shared. That's why we host regular events, support local artists, and create a space where connections happen naturally.
